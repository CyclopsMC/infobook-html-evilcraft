Blood Pearl of Teleportation

Regular Ender Pearls are fun for teleporting around, but this can get quite expensive. Using the powers of Blood, I was able to construct a special Ender Pearl that is completely reusable at the cost of some Blood. It also seems much more potent than regular Ender Pearls, which can only be good.
