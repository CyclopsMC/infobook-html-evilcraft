Effortless Ring

I was able to construct a special ring that can apply the Promises of §Productivity§0 and Velocity which allows you to walk faster, jump higher and step more easily on blocks. Just hold this item anywhere inside your inventory to gain its effects. Note that this step assist can be avoided by holding Shift.
