Broom Modifiers

In this section, I will list all modifier effects I have discovered during my adventures. They can be applied to an existing Broom by combining it in any crafting grid. Once the modifier is applied, there is no way to remove it again from the Broom.

Modifier Count
The maximum amount of modifiers a Broom can have.

Speed
The top speed of a Broom.

Acceleration
The higher this value, the faster the Broom will accelerate.

Maneuverability
How fast a Broom can rotate.

Levitation
The higher this value, the faster the Broom can fly up.

Damage
The damage that will be afflicted onto colliding mobs. The faster the Broom is flying, the higher this damage will be.

Particles
How much particles should be emitted from the Broom when flying.

Flame
Any colliding entity will be lit on fire.

Smash
This is used to enable the Broom to break blocks when flying. Higher values can break harder blocks.

Bouncy
Colliding entities will be bounced away, depending on this modifier value.

Witherer
The wither effect will be applied to colliding entities.

Hungerer
Colliding entities will experience sudden hunger.

Kamikaze
When colliding with an entity the Broom will explode. It is very unlikely that a mounted player would survive something like this.

Wither Shield
The higher the modifier value, the larger the chance that the mounted player will be protected from incoming projectiles.

Thoughness
Higher modifier values will make the Broom less influencable by external factors during movement.

Efficiency
A higher modifier value will result in a higher chance that nood Blood needs to be consumed.

Swimming
This makes the Broom better maneuverable in liquids.

Icy
Slows down colliding entities.
