101の魂大殺戮

次々と魂を処理していくうちに、私は彼らに囲まれていても非常に落ち着いていられるようになった。 そのため私は彼らについていくつかの特徴と、等しく何かに恐れている事に気づいた！ 彼らはどんな形であれ、'終焉'に関係することを嫌うようだ。

I was always annoyed by the fact that those nasty spirits could attack me, and there was nothing I could do against them. That is why I investigated into a way to damage them. It looks like their fear of endings was grounded, because those things damage them. Using some 'end'-relics, I was able to transform my Vengeance Focus into an improved version which is able to damage them.

This new focus appeared to be strong enough to completely 'kill' any spirit. But to my surprise, they drop some stuff. This includes some things that their old physical form carried and also some ghastly dust, which seems to carry the spirits' darkest parts and may prove useful in the future...
