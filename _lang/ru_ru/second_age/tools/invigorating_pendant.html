Бодрящий кулон

Getting rid of bad potion effects can be a pain. This pendant is able to remove any bad effect at the cost of some Blood. Worse effects cost more Blood. It even seems to extinguish fire, but I wouldn’t recommend swimming in lava just yet. You will need a special kind of string to craft this, which can be created by combining regular string with a small fragment of gold.
