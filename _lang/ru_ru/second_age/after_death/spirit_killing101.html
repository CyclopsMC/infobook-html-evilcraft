Убийство-духов 101

After handling spirits one after the other, I became quite comfortable with being among them. Because of that, I was able to notice some of their specific traits, and even fears! They seem to dislike anything having to do with 'endings' in any form.

I was always annoyed by the fact that those nasty spirits could attack me, and there was nothing I could do against them. That is why I investigated a way to damage them. It looks like their fear of endings was grounded, because those things damage them. Using some 'end'-relics, I was able to transform my Vengeance Focus to an improved version which is able to damage them.

This new focus appeared to be strong enough to completely 'kill' any spirit. But to my suprise, they drop some stuff. This includes some things that their old physical form carried and also some ghastly dust, which seems to carry the spirits' darkest parts and may prove useful in the future...
