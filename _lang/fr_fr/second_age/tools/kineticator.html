Kineticator

This item is able to attract items and experience orbs in a certain area. This area can be changed by right-clicking it, enabling or disabling it is done with a simple shift + right-click. Items that are attracted from nearby consumes little to no Blood, but items from further away will require some more Blood. Sneaking will avoid kineticating items.

Reversing the recipe will give the opposite version of this item which is able to repel items and experience orbs.
