Sanguinary Pedestal

I was getting tired of pushing mobs off of cliffs and extracting the Blood, that is why I constructed a device which is able to extract Blood from nearby stained blocks and place it in any nearby tanks. Using some Dark Power Gems, the Blood efficiency of this Blood extraction can be increased.

Placing a Spiked Plate on top of it will enhance the capabilities of this plate to also absorb the Blood of the beasts it kills and redirect it to the pedestal.
