Primed Pendant

After making the Invigorating Pendant, I started thinking. What if it would be possible to not only remove, but also apply effects? This new pendant is able to hold one Potion, and by using Blood it will continuously apply its effect to you. I noticed that some effects cost more Blood than the others. You can insert new Potions by simply right-clicking with the item.
