Biome Extract

Our world is made up of Biomes, many different variantions exist. Unfortunately, we can not choose the location of these Biomes, or at least not always.

One of the abilities of the Environmental Accumulator is that it can accept special Biome Extract Bottles which it can fill with the Biome the machine is currently placed in. These bottles can be thrown anywhere on the ground to transform that area to the given biome.

One major disadvantage of the Environmental Accumulator is that it will degrade its biome over time, so the bottles coming out of it will become pretty useless. If only there was an alternative way to craft these Biome Extracts without bad effects...
