Gemstone Torch

I was able to combine the Burning Gemstone with a Dark Stick to make a special torch that seems to absorb any spawning Vengeance Spirits in its light area. Note that any spirits spawned outside the area will still be able to wander in this area though, but a simple mundane wall should be able to solve that.
