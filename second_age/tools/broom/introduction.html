Broom Basics

Brooms are typically used to clean the floor. I noticed however, that by creating them using Crushed Dark Gems and Dark Sticks, and infusing them with a constant stream of Blood, they seem to gain some very interesting qualities. When handling a Broom in a certain way, it seems to float. After practicing with it for a while, I was able to control it enough to be able to fly in any direction I wanted while sitting on it.

Brooms are made of three main parts. The Rod is the basis of your broom, this will influence its main properties. A Brush is needed to make the Broom maneuvarable. Finally, a Cap is needed to balance your Broom.

All possible Broom parts must be created using the Bare Rod, Brush and Cap, this can be done by combining these three parts of any material in any crafting grid. Parts made from different materials seem to have different properties. All possible properties will be explained in the following section after that I will give an overview of all Broom parts.

When flying with the Broom, make sure that it has enough Blood at all times, otherwise the Broom will slow down significantly.
