Purifier

After the unfortunate side-effects of the Blood Chest, I decided to design a new machine that is able to remove these bad effects from your tools. Simply by placing a tool with a bad effect inside of this block together with at least one bucket worth of Blood will remove this effect from the item.

An unintended benefit of this machine is that when filling it completely with Blood and placing an enchanted item together with a Blook (which is just a Book infused with Blood), will move a random enchantment from the item to the book, at the cost of three Buckets of Blood.

Recently, I noticed that this machine also allows you to remove Potion Effects from living creatures sitting inside of it by filling the Purifier completely with Blood and giving it an Empty Bottle. After a few seconds, the effect will be contained inside the bottle and the creature will be freed of it.
